# League Options

Format:
- Ottoneu
- Custom

Scoring:
- 5 x 5
  Batters: R, RBI, SB, HR, AVG
  Pitchers: W, SV, ERA, WHIP, K
- 4 x 4
  Batters: OBP, SLG, HR, R
  Pitchers: ERA, WHIP, HR/9, K
- Points
  - Fangraphs: FanGraphs Points in https://ottoneu.fangraphs.com/support
  - SABR: See 
- Custom
  - Use any percentage or counting stat mix for n x n
  - Define your own points algorithm

Options:
- Head to Head
  - Playoffs option
    - 2 teams, one week
    - 4 teams, two weeks
    - 6 or 8 teams, three weeks
    - custom

Roster Size:
- Ottoneu = 40
- Custom
  - 23 is common for redraft formats

Roster Slots:
- Ottoneu = 2 x C, 1B, 2B, 3B, SS, MI, 5 x OF, 5 x SP, 5 x RP
- Custom
  - Different formats have different slot counts
  - Some formats isolate LF, CF, RF
  - Some formats have CI

Teams
- 12 is default
- 10, 12, 14, 20 common
- Custom
